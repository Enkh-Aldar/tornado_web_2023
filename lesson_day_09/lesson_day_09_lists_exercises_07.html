Impossible quiche recipe

  • Ingredients
    • 125g ham, chopped
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 1 1/2 cups (180g) grated tasty cheese
    • 1/3 cup (50g) self-raising flour
    • Salt & freshly ground pepper, to season
    • 4 eggs
    • 1 1/2 cups (375ml) milk
  • Steps
    • Preheat oven to 200#8451. Grease a 5 cup capacity pie dish.
    • Combine ham, onion, cheese, flour, salt and pepper in a medium bowl. Scatter over base of dish.
    • Whisk eggs and mimlk together in a large jug and pour over ham mixture. Cook for about 40 minutes until puffed and golden. Cool slightly. Serve warm or cold.
